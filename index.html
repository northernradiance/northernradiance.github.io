"Capture your special day with a touch of radiance"

Welcome, and thank you for visiting! Northern Radiance is a friendly, hometown company with a passion for the media we create. We have filmed countless weddings for over five years up and down the east coast. We are based out of Bryson, QC and we also offer our services in both English and French!

What sets us apart?

We're your one-stop wedding shop! Not only do we offer wedding top-notch videography, we offer packages including invitation designs, wedding party gift sets, and more! Check out the packages page for more information!

We don't set an hourly limit, we film for your entire wedding day: no questions asked! We shoot beautifully cinematic footage with professional 4K cameras. We offer in-depth, in-person consulations to make sure we film and deliver your wedding media exactly as you picture it.

We currently serve the greater Ottawa/Pembroke/Renfrew areas, but we are absolutely willing to expand our network. Please contact us for specifics.
